# Cellular Overview

KORLINX cellular modules deliver reliable wide-area IoT connectivity over licensed cellular spectrum — no private infrastructure required.

## Cellular IoT Technologies

| Technology | Bandwidth | Max Speed | Power Profile | Mobility | Typical Use |
|------------|-----------|-----------|---------------|----------|-------------|
| **LTE-M** | 1.4 MHz | 1 Mbps DL | Low (PSM/eDRX) | Full | Mobile trackers, wearables |
| **NB-IoT** | 180 kHz | 250 kbps DL | Ultra-low | Limited | Fixed meters, sensors |
| **LTE Cat-1** | 5/10 MHz | 10 Mbps DL | Medium | Full | Gateways, POS terminals |
| **5G NR** | Up to 100 MHz | Gbps class | Variable | Full | High-speed industrial IoT |

:::tip LTE-M vs NB-IoT
Both operate in licensed spectrum and support PSM/eDRX sleep modes. Choose **LTE-M** when you need voice, higher data rates, or device mobility. Choose **NB-IoT** for static, ultra-low-power sensor deployments.
:::

## Key Features of KORLINX Cellular Modules

- **Plug-and-Play** — AT command interface over UART, USB, RS232, or RS485
- **Global Band Support** — multi-band designs cover North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific
- **SIM Flexibility** — nano SIM + optional eSIM on select models
- **Security** — TLS 1.2/1.3, DTLS, and hardware root of trust
- **Cloud Ready** — built-in MQTT, CoAP, HTTP(S) stacks
