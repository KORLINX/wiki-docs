# Cellular Overview
<div>
<p>Building something that needs to phone home from the middle of nowhere? A device going into a basement, a moving truck, or a field with no Wi-Fi for miles? That's exactly where cellular IoT shines. Unlike Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, you don't need a router nearby — and unlike LoRaWAN, you don't have to build and babysit your own gateways. Your device connects to the same cellular networks your phone uses, anywhere in the world, the moment you power it on.</p>
<p>That's the thinking behind Korlinx: Plug-and-Play IoT Solutions that work the second you switch them on. Our all-in-one single modules pack the modem, GNSS, MCU, and security into one tiny package — no fragmented chips, no complex board design. And because it's built for low-power cellular IoT, your device can run for years on a single battery, out in the field, untouched. Drop in a module, power it up, and you're connected. If your project needs to go places and simply work, Korlinx is the fastest way to get there.</p>
</div>
