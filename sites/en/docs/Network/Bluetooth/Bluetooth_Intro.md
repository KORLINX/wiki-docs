# Bluetooth Overview

KORLINX Bluetooth modules support Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) 5.x and Bluetooth Mesh — enabling low-power, short-range sensor networks and device-to-gateway links.

## Bluetooth Technologies

| Technology | Range | Data Rate | Topology | Power | Use Case |
|------------|-------|-----------|----------|-------|----------|
| **BLE 5.0** | 40–400 m (LR mode) | 125 kbps–2 Mbps | Point-to-point | Ultra-low | Sensors, wearables |
| **BLE 5.1** | Same + AoA/AoD | Same | P2P + localization | Ultra-low | RTLS, positioning |
| **BLE 5.2** | Same + LE Audio | Same | P2P + broadcast | Ultra-low | Audio, hearing aids |
| **BT Mesh** | 100 m per hop | 1 Mbps | Many-to-many mesh | Low | Smart buildings, lighting |

---

## BLE 5.x Highlights

- **2× speed** at 2 Mbps for faster file transfer
- **4× range** in Long Range (Coded PHY) mode
- **8× advertising capacity** with extended advertising
- **Angle of Arrival/Departure** (5.1+) for sub-meter indoor positioning
- **LE Audio & LC3** (5.2+) for high-quality low-latency audio streaming

---

## Bluetooth Mesh

Bluetooth Mesh creates self-healing, many-to-many networks suitable for:
- Building automation (lighting, HVAC, access control)
- Industrial sensor networks
- Smart city infrastructure
