# WiFi Overview

KORLINX WiFi modules support the full IEEE 802.11 family — from classic 802.11n (WiFi 4) to the latest 802.11ax (WiFi 6) and the long-range 802.11ah (WiFi HaLow) standard.

## WiFi Standards

| Standard | Generation | Max Speed | Frequency | Range | Key Feature |
|----------|------------|-----------|-----------|-------|-------------|
| **802.11n** | WiFi 4 | 600 Mbps | 2.4 / 5 GHz | ~70 m | MIMO, widely supported |
| **802.11ac** | WiFi 5 | 3.5 Gbps | 5 GHz | ~35 m | MU-MIMO, beamforming |
| **802.11ax** | WiFi 6/6E | 9.6 Gbps | 2.4 / 5 / 6 GHz | ~30 m | OFDMA, WPA3, high density |
| **802.11ah** | WiFi HaLow | 347 Mbps | Sub-1 GHz | ~1 km | Long range, low power |

:::tip WiFi HaLow for IoT
WiFi HaLow (802.11ah) operates in the sub-1 GHz band, providing **kilometer-range** coverage with significantly lower power consumption than standard WiFi — ideal for large-scale outdoor IoT deployments.
:::

## Security

All KORLINX WiFi modules support:
- **WPA3 Personal and Enterprise** (where applicable)
- **TLS 1.3** for MQTT, HTTPS, and WebSocket
- Certificate-based device authentication
