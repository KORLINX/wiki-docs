# Network Overview

KORLINX provides IoT networking modules and solutions across six major connectivity technologies — from global cellular networks to low-power local radios.

## Technology Categories

| Technology | Standards | Range | Power | Best For |
|------------|-----------|-------|-------|----------|
| [Cellular](./Cellular/Cellular_Intro) | LTE-M, NB-IoT, LTE Cat-1, 5G | Global | Low–Med | Mobile assets, outdoor sensors |
| [LPWAN](./LPWAN/LPWAN_Intro) | LoRaWAN, Sigfox, Zigbee | km–city scale | Ultra-low | Battery-powered sensors |
| [WiFi](./WiFi/WiFi_Intro) | WiFi 4/5/6, WiFi HaLow | 10–1000 m | Medium | High-bandwidth local |
| [Bluetooth](./Bluetooth/Bluetooth_Intro) | BLE 5.x, Mesh | 10–100 m | Ultra-low | Sensor networks, beacons |
| [Ethernet](./Ethernet/Ethernet_Intro) | Industrial, PoE | Wired | Medium | Reliable wired infrastructure |
| [Satellite](./Satellite/Satellite_Intro) | LEO, GEO | Global | High | Remote, off-grid deployments |

---

## Choosing a Technology

### Wide-Area Coverage
- **Cellular** — carrier-grade reliability, broad roaming support, ideal for mobile or critical assets.
- **LPWAN** — years of battery life for low-data sensors (meters, trackers, agriculture).
- **Satellite** — anywhere on Earth when cellular is unavailable.

### Short-Range / Local
- **WiFi** — high throughput for gateways, cameras, and data-rich sensors.
- **Bluetooth** — mesh or direct link for sensor-to-gateway hops.
- **Ethernet** — industrial wired backbone for factories and infrastructure.
